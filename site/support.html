Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app in Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you need assistance with account deletion, contact us at jared@rustpoint.ai.

How do I export my data?

We support data portability. To request an export of your personal data, email us at jared@rustpoint.ai with the subject line "Data Export Request." We will provide your data within 30 days.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email jared@rustpoint.ai with:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if applicable

We take bug reports seriously and aim to address them in the next app update.

Do you offer refunds?

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. For refund requests, please use Apple's standard refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com. If you have questions about your purchase, we're happy to help — just email us.

How do I contact support?

The best way to reach us is via email at jared@rustpoint.ai. We typically respond within 48 hours on business days.
